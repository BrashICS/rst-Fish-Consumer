HELLO

This page will go over what the game objects do and the general controls for the game



Controls:
  • W,A,S,D to move UP,LEFT,DOWN and RIGHT respectivly
  • press f to interact with stations

Stations:


  • These purple squares represent walls and cannot be walked through. They are basicly just duds that do nothing

  • Blue squares give food when interacted with and gives cheese.

  • Red squares are grills that allow to grill cheese. Not make grilled cheese, grill cheese. Just you tossing raw cheese on a grill and then serving that to customers.

  • You serve the "Grilled Cheese" to customers which awards you points.

  • The button in the bottom left of the game with start the game. Allowing you to interact with stations and spawns projectiles.

  • these bouncing gray cubes will end the game if they touch you. They will turn red before their last bounce and will go much faster when it is their last bounce.
